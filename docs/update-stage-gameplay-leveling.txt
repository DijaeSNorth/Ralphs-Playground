Stage: Gameplay XP leveling

Summary:
- Creatures now level through normal play, not only Steroids.
- Active crew gains XP from successful captures, workouts, boss battles, and completed goals.
- Newly captured creatures receive a small capture XP bonus when added to active crew or storage.
- XP thresholds now scale by level through getBuddyXpForNextLevel().
- Level-ups improve strength, endurance, focus, and energy.
- Steroids still instantly add one level and keep their stronger stat bump.
- Boss events now trigger save so boss XP/rewards persist.
- Crew and storage cards show XP progress bars.

Important files:
- src/game/simulation/world.ts: XP curve, XP awards, level-up helper, capture/workout/boss/goal rewards.
- src/ui/hud.ts: imports XP curve helper and renders XP bars.
- src/style.css: retro XP bar styling.
- src/render/app/createGymBuddyGame.ts: includes boss events in auto-save trigger.
