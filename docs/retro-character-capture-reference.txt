Retro character and capture redesign reference

Files changed:
- src/render/objects/lowPolyFactory.ts

Rendering approach:
- Uses simplified 2.5D chunky geometry rather than billboard textures.
- Player is now a compact retro trainer silhouette with flat body pieces, cap, bold outline, and shadow blob.
- Wild creatures are now built as flattened, outline-backed shape stacks with exaggerated chest/arms.
- Exotic creatures retain larger scale and aura treatment.

Readable creature cues:
- Buff Bunny: long ears and compact strong body.
- Bench Bear: round ears, snout, wide chest.
- Flex Fox: pointed ears, tail, agile silhouette.
- Iron Rhino: forward horn and thick chest.
- Pump Panther: cat ears, sleek tail, accent chest.
- Swole Gorilla: round ears, huge hanging arms, broad shoulders.
- Minotaur Maximus: horns and bulky torso.
- Griffin Gains: wings, beak, pointed head features.
- Dragon Deadlift: wings, tail, horns.
- Cyclops Curl: single large eye and curling arm.

Capture presentation:
- Arm-wrestle arm mesh is now a chunky flat retro prop.
- Capture platform is now a flat rectangular battle mat instead of a torus/projectile-style ring.
- Crying reaction uses larger cartoon blue drops for 2.5D readability.

Systems preserved:
- Capture odds, capture events, roster/RepDex, save/load, steroids, goals, tutorial, and spawning systems remain unchanged.
