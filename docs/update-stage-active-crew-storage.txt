Stage: Active crew slots and creature storage

Summary:
- Active crew remains capped at 4 creatures through MAX_ROSTER_SIZE.
- Captures now join active crew only when a slot is open; otherwise they go to storage.
- Stored creatures stay owned, persist in save data, count for RepDex, and can be renamed, released, or leveled with Steroids.
- HUD crew management now has separate Active Crew and Storage sections.
- Players can store ready active creatures and activate stored creatures when an active slot is available.
- Passive abilities, workouts, boss calculations, and field crew behavior use active crew only.

Important files:
- src/game/types.ts: WorldSnapshot includes storage.
- src/game/progress.ts: SavedProgress includes storage and old saves default safely.
- src/game/simulation/world.ts: owns active roster/storage, capture destination, save/load, store/activate actions.
- src/ui/hud.ts: renders active crew and storage lists, detail card, and store/activate/release controls.
- src/render/app/createGymBuddyGame.ts: wires HUD store/activate callbacks to world actions.
- src/style.css: styles the retro active/storage crew management panels.
